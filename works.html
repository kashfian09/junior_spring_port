Unity

Monkey Business! is one of the first games I made in my sophomore year. You play as Froggalina and roll to collect all the bananas before George the monkey can eat them all! It was my first ever submission to Games For Change and it was a huge learning curve for me in 3D game development, since it was my first time using C#.

Skills: C#, Unity, Perseverance

Space Blaster! is a more recent project of mine. It’s a sci-fi take on road safety where you ‘drive’ in outer space and fend against aliens! Another submission to Games For Change, it was my first time trying to build a 2D game. I had a lot of fun making the maps for this one, and overall just had a blast (pun intended)!

Skills: C#, Unity, Diligence

Rainforest Ranger to the Rescue! is my latest release. It is a 2D platformer where you play as Randy, putting out fires, collecting seed samples, and dodging venomous snakes! It's a game I'm really proud of, as despite making it in a time crunch, the UI turned out way better than I expected.

Skills: C#, Unity, Tile Map, Resourcefulness
