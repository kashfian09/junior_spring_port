Everyone knows good things come in threes!

Monkey Business! is one of the first games I made in my sophomore year. You play as Froggalina and roll to collect all the bananas before George the monkey can eat them all!

Space Blaster! is a more recent project of mine. It’s a sci-fi take on road safety where you ‘drive’ in outer space and fend against aliens!

Rainforest Ranger to the Rescue! is my latest release. It is a 2D platformer where you play as Randy, putting out fires, collecting seed samples, and dodging venomous snakes!
